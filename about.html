The Woman
Behind 3K

With a Red Seal qualification in spray painting and over 7 years of hands-on experience, Kelebogile Mothelesi didn't just enter the automotive industry — she transformed it. Affectionately known as "Mma Dipente" (Mother of Paint), her nickname carries the deep respect she commands through artistry, precision, and an unrelenting standard of excellence.

My hands mix paint, but my heart mixes purpose. Every car tells a story — I make sure the finish is unforgettable.

— Kelebogile Mothelesi, Founder

Born from a passion for rural development and female empowerment, Kelebogile founded 3K Auto Body Repairs to create a space where high-end craftsmanship meets real social impact. Every vehicle that leaves her workshop carries not only a flawless finish but a legacy of breaking barriers in a male-dominated trade.

The Path of Mastery

2016
Journey Begins

Kelebogile starts her apprenticeship in spray painting, discovering an innate talent for precision finishing and an obsession with colour accuracy that sets her apart from the start.

Mastery Compounds

Years of relentless practice across panel beating, advanced colour matching, and custom airbrushing. She earns the nickname "Mma Dipente" on the workshop floor.

2018–20
2021
Red Seal Achievement

Earns the prestigious Red Seal qualification — the highest standard in South Africa's trades. She becomes one of a rare few female Red Seal spray painters in the country.

3K Auto Body Founded

Launches her own shop with a bold vision: blend automotive excellence with rural development and female empowerment. 3K becomes a beacon for quality and community impact.
