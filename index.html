About Us

The Soul
Behind 3K

Kelebogile Mothelesi — known as "Mma Dipente", the Mother of Paint — is a young Black female entrepreneur who holds a Red Seal certification in spray painting with over 7 years of hands-on mastery.

Her journey from rural roots to master craftswoman is baked into every repair. At 3K Auto Body, we don't just fix cars — we restore confidence, uplift communities, and deliver world-class panel beating and refinishing with a personal signature.

Our commitment to rural empowerment and female leadership in the trades sets us apart. Every vehicle we touch carries a piece of that story.
