    Questions

    ✦Do you work with insurance claims?

    Yes — we assist with all insurance paperwork and work directly with major South African insurers for seamless, stress-free repairs.

    ✦How long does a full respray take?

    Typically 3–7 working days depending on damage scope, colour complexity, and whether structural repairs are required.

    ✦Do you offer warranties on paintwork?

    Absolutely. All spray painting services include a comprehensive warranty against defects in materials and workmanship.

    ✦Can you match any car colour?

    Yes — with advanced spectrophotometer technology and Mma Dipente's Red Seal expertise, we match any factory or custom colour including matte, pearl, and metallic.

    ✦Do you offer mobile assessments?

    Yes, mobile assessments are available within the Gauteng region. Contact us to arrange a convenient time and location.
